About

Hello! I'm Pratosh R, a dedicated and imaginative person with a strong background in design and wallarts. My goal is to produce incredible experiences that are memorable. I'm passionate for learning new skills in design tools and I personally feel like my interest towards human psychology helps me a lot in handling design challenges. I've refined my approach over the course of my career, always searching for ways to improve processes and deliver measurable outcomes. As a task-oriented and strategic thinker, I take pride in my ability to utilise industry best practises to deliver the best results.

I appreciate collaborating with various teams to develop revolutionary, ground-breaking concepts and do well in hectic settings. My enthusiasm for is what drives me to want to push the limits of creativity and to help growth. I'm interested in psychology of Human interactions towards modern world and want to be a part of it. Recently I got inspired by the 21st Century Design by Don Norman impacted my perspective towards Design thinking.
